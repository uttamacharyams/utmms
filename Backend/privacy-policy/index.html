Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

Marriage Station Pvt. Ltd. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Marriage Station ("App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

📌 Key Points:
  • We collect personal information to provide matchmaking services
  • Your data is never sold to third parties
  • You have full control to delete your account anytime
  • We use industry-standard security measures

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • Date of birth, gender, age
  • Marital status, religion, caste, community
  • Education, occupation, annual income
  • Location (city, state, country)
  • Family details (family type, family values)
  • Lifestyle information (diet, drinking/smoking habits)
  • Partner preferences (age range, religion, education, etc.)
  • Profile photos and gallery images
  • Government ID for verification (optional)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent, interactions
  • IP Address: For security and analytics
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Match you with compatible profiles based on your preferences
  • Facilitate communication between users (chats, requests)
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Improve our matchmaking algorithms and app features
  • Send important notifications about matches, messages, and requests
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your profile information (name, age, location, photos, preferences) is visible to other registered users as part of the matchmaking service.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services for hosting, analytics, and push notifications. These providers only access data necessary to perform their services.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy, your information may be transferred.
⚠️ Important: Your contact information (email, phone) is hidden by default. Other users can only contact you through the app's chat feature. You have control over who can see your profile photos.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls restrict employee access to user data

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the app settings. Account deletion is instant and permanent. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 24 hours. To delete:

  • Go to Settings → Delete Account
  • Provide a reason for deletion (optional)
  • Confirm deletion

5.2 Privacy Settings

You can control who sees your profile photos:

  • All Users: Anyone can view your photos
  • Premium Users Only: Only paid members can view
  • Verified Users Only: Only verified members can view
  • Private: Photos are blurred; users must request access

5.3 Blocking Users

You can block any user from your profile menu. Blocked users cannot message you or view your profile. You can manage blocked users in Settings → Blocked Users.

5.4 Reporting Users

If you encounter inappropriate behavior, you can report the user from their profile. Our team reviews reports within 24-48 hours.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted within 24 hours. Some anonymized data may be retained for analytics purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for adults aged 18 years and above. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18. If we discover a user under 18, we will delete their account immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending a notification to registered users

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
